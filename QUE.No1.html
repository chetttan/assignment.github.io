Tomato Bacon Grilled Cheese:

A yummy meal that you and your kids will love! A simple golden brown grilled cheese sandwich with the twist of bacon and tomato. Hope you enjoy!









Chicken sandwich mixed with mayonnaise, crunchy veggies, shredded boneless chicken, and other spices. This tasty chicken filling is spread on the bread slice, sprinkle with some grated cheese, and then grilled. This cheesy chicken sandwich full fills chicken lovers.

Ingredients:

Ingredients for chicken sandwich:

  1. Boneless chicken – 250 g

  2. Bay leaves – 2

  3. pepper powder – 1/4 teaspoon

  4. Salt – 1/2 teaspoon

  5. Ginger and garlic paste – 1 tablespoon

  6. Oil – 1 tablespoon

  7. Water – 1 cup

  8. Ghee – 1 tablespoon

  9. Chopped garlic – 1 teaspoon

  10. Chopped onions – 1/2 cup

  11. Grated carrot – 1 cup

  12. Chopped capsicum – 1 cup

  13. Black pepper powder – 1/2 teaspoon

  14. Garam masala powder – 1/4 teaspoon

  15. Salt – 1/4 teaspoon

  16. Chopped coriander leaves – 1/2 cup

  17. Tomato sauce – 1 tablespoon

  18. Red chilli sauce – 1 tablespoon

  19. Mayonnaise – 2 1/2 tablespoons

  20. Grated cheese – 3 tablespoons

  21. Butter – 1/2 teaspoon

instruction:

  1. Add 250 g boneless chicken to the pan.
  2. Add 2 bay leaves, 1/4 teaspoon of pepper powder, and 12 teaspoon of salt.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger and garlic paste, 1 tablespoon of oil, and a cup of water.
  4. Mix well, cover, and boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir occasionally and dry it completely.
  6. Let it cool shred the chicken pieces into thin strips.
  7. Take shredded chicken into a bowl and set aside.
